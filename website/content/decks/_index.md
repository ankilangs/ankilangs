## About These Decks

Each deck includes:

- **Audio**: Native speaker pronunciation for every word
- **IPA**: International Phonetic Alphabet transcriptions
- **Four card types**: Pronunciation, Listening, Reading, and Spelling
- **Regular updates**: Improvements and additions released frequently
- **Open source**: Licensed under permissive licenses for easy reuse

All decks are available on [GitHub Releases](https://github.com/ankilangs/ankilangs/releases) and [AnkiWeb](https://ankiweb.net/shared/by-author/673955494).
