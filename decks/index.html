      Available Decks#

      All decks are free, open source, and actively developed. Each deck includes audio pronunciation, IPA transcriptions, and four card types to reinforce learning.

      Note: All decks are currently in development and may not yet contain all 625 words. See individual deck pages for current status.

      About These Decks#

      Each deck includes:

      • Audio: Native speaker pronunciation for every word
      • IPA: International Phonetic Alphabet transcriptions
      • Four card types: Pronunciation, Listening, Reading, and Spelling
      • Regular updates: Improvements and additions released frequently
      • Open source: Licensed under permissive licenses for easy reuse

      All decks are available on GitHub Releases and AnkiWeb.
